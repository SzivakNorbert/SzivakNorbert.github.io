Hi,

 I am Norbert Szivak, 21 years old, a university student. I became interested in the world of IT at a young age, and we can say that it has always been a part of my life.
  I finished elementary school in a quiet little village in the Hungary lowlands. After I finished elementary school, we moved further west, to Koszeg.
  I went to a technical high school here, where I studied logistics. I became interested in programming at the end of my vocational high school years, and I fell in love with it.
  In my free time, I just studied and studied, but I felt that it would be quite difficult for me to acquire this knowledge myself.
 That's when I decided to try university. I wrote an advanced IT final exam, which I did very well, and that year I started university life at the ELTE SEK Faculty of Informatics, as a Computer Scientist.
